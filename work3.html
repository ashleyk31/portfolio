Introduction

With over one billion users, TikTok is arguably one of the most pop- ular social media platforms of all time (Duffy, 2023). The app itself functions as follows: each user has an individual account/profile and can create videos to share with both their followers or friends, but those videos also can reach individuals with whom the user has no literal connection. Users can like, comment on, and share videos with others (Zhang, 2023). The “For You Page” allows users to view videos curated for them. TikTok uses a specific and unique algo- rithm to introduce the videos on a user’s “For You Page”— further individualizing user experiences. TikTok’s immense success has led to other popular platforms integrating features originally unique to TikTok; YouTube Shorts and Instagram’s Reels both closely resemble the user experience on TikTok. However, they arguably fall short of capturing its true essence, as they act more as secondary content forms to boost platform engagement than TikTok that uses them as a primary content form. Further, brands, influencers, and celebrities have utilized TikTok to promote their content. Given the extreme popularity and influence of the application and its relevance to individuals and companies looking to expand their audiences and increase their popularity, it is important to understand the factors that determine a video’s virality on the platform. This understand- ing can potentially provide valuable insights for content creators on how to optimize video details such as video descriptions to increase the likelihood of virality when it comes to dance trend videos on TikTok. The decision to analyze specifically dance trends comes from the platform’s popularity and market di!erentiation from other social media platforms due to viral dance trends started by artists and influencers on the platform during COVID-19 lockdowns. Since most were at home and had limited access to outside entertainment, many households found joy in watching and recreating these dances to the original sounds they were posted to. Now, beyond the lock- down, TikTok dance trends continue to go viral and are now used by content creators and brands (not just to entertain their audience) but to promote their content and businesses. Therefore, insights into how these trends become viral could be game-changers in the world of TikTok content creation, o!ering new strategies and approaches for maximizing the reach and impact of dance trend videos. For our study, we investigated the patterns that influence the virality of TikTok videos and how this changes over time? To answer this question, we looked into two questions and hypothesized what would happen in the study: RQ1: What distinguishes the accounts driving the accelerated virality of trends? RQ2: How do specific trends in video descriptions contribute to the virality of TikTok videos?
