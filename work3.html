A Quantitative Analysis of TikTok Dance Trends' Viral Success


Wellesley College - Data Science and the Web Spring 2024

Tools
Selenium Linear Regression Analysis Time Series Pandas Seaborn Matplotlib


Introduction

With over one billion users, TikTok functions as the following: each user has an individual account/profile and can create videos to share with both their followers or friends, but those videos also can reach individuals with whom the user has no literal connection. Further, brands, influencers, and celebrities have utilized TikTok to promote their content. And given the extreme popularity and influence of the application and its relevance to individuals and companies looking to expand their audiences and increase their popularity, it is important to understand the factors that determine a videoâ€™s virality on the platform.

So we aimed to investigate the patterns that influence the virality of TikTok videos and how this changes over time.
To answer this question, we looked into two questions and hypothesized what would happen in the study:

Research Question 1: What distinguishes the accounts driving the accelerated virality of trends?
Research Question 2: How do specific trends in video descriptions contribute to the virality of TikTok videos?

Role : Data Analyst, Data Scientist

The project consisted of six members. We collaborated throughout the entire process of brainstorming, data collecting, and data analyzing for a month. Ultimately, we presented our findings in front of the class.



My Contributions

I was responsible for conducting the first hypothesis, starting with scraping data from TikTok. I filtered the dataset to identify the top 5 songs for each year and categorized the influencers based on our predefined tier scale. Additionally, I created a time series for each song, with the frequency representing the total view count over time.
