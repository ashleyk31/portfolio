Introduction

Conducted statistical tests to support our hypothesis of a presence of correlation between the amount of CO2 a country emits and the percentage of women in their parliament



My Contribution

I played a key role in the data cleaning process by identifying and addressing missing data. This involved checking for missing values and determining the best methods to handle them. Additionally, I removed duplicates to ensure data integrity. Our project utilized two datasets, which I successfully merged for comprehensive analysis. Furthermore, I was responsible for writing the research background and conducting ANOVA and regression analyses to obtain the final results, including the necessary p-values.
