[ STORY ]

I developed an interactive 3D recreation of the video game Crossy Road using Three.js, exploring dynamic camera perspectives and real-time scene controls. This project successfully reimagines a top-down mobile game from multiple viewpoints, creating an immersive experience that highlights technical skill in 3D graphics programming and spatial design. My role as Developer and Engineer focused on scene composition, texture mapping, and interactive controls. This case study emphasizes the creative decisions and technical implementations I led from concept to final render.
